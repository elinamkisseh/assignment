Before we jump to the cool and fun sports, here are some common terms we must know and understand so don't get confused when we are at the event.

*Player: Someone participating intensively in a sporting activity.

*Supporter: A person who is actively interested in and wishes succes for a particular sport team.

*Referee: An official who watches a game closely to ensure that the rules are adhered to and to arbitrate on matters arising from the play.

Spectators: A person who watches the play for fun.

Stadium: An athletic or sports ground with tiers of seats for spectators.
